Target Users

Our primary targets are local businesses and consumers who want sustainable packaging alternatives.

Solution

  1. Our Prototype:

    Our prototype, called DOST green packaging, offers an eco-friendly solution to plastic bags that are boidegradable, meaning they break down naturally and do not harm the environment, In addition, we created seed-embedded paper packaging that can be planted after use. instead of becoming waste, the packaging turns

  2. How to use our prototype:

    1. Use like a normal packaging.

    2. After use, don’t throw it in the trash. Seed paper is biodegradable and can grow plants.

    3. Plant it in soil. Put the used paper directly into soil, garden, or pot, and do not bury it too deep.

    4. Water regularly. Keep the soil moist but not soggy.

    5. Watch the plant grow. Continue watering and caring for the plants.
